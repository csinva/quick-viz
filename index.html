Quick Quiz

Learn pairs of things, one CSV at a time.

Sporcle-style typing quizzes generated from CSV files. Pick a topic to start, or bring your own data.

Add a quiz: drop a CSV into quiz/data/, register it in quiz/data.js with a defaultPrompt and defaultAnswer column, and it'll appear here. Optional columns: Aliases (pipe-separated alternates per row), plus any low-cardinality column (≤30 distinct values) automatically becomes a filter.
